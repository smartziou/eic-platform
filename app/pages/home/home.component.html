A registry of text and data mining services

A place to find and share TDM services where...
  • Researchers are able to find the most fitting tools for their work.
  • Text and data miners share their components, tools and services.
  • Application developers mix and match NLP components to build TDM applications.

A bridge to OA scientific and scholarly literature

A hassle-free access and mechanism where...
  • Researchers are able to find or build corpora from OA scientific and scholarly literature data sources.
  • Content providers put forward their content to be further and intelligently used in research.

A cloud computing environment

A trusted and connected environment where...
  • Researchers are able to utilize computing resources to seamlessly run TDM on OA content.
  • Researchers share and publish annotated corpora as research results.
  • Application developers build and evaluate more complex NLP related workflows.
